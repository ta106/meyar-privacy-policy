Account and Data Deletion

Meyar allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Please send an email to: turki.harbi@hotmail.com

Include the email address used to register your Meyar account.

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information (email, user ID)
  • Submitted requests and associated audio recordings

Retention period

Account and data deletion will be completed within 30 days of request.
